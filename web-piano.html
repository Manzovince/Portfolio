Web Piano

    Arduino

It started with a simple idea: What if the piano keys I play could light up a strip of LEDs in real time?

At the time, I had never touched an Arduino, never flashed an ESP32, and barely knew what MIDI looked like under the hood. I just wanted to visualize what I was playing to make music feel a bit more alive, more tangible.

So I gave it a shot.
